Our Comprehensive Defense Services

A multi-faceted approach to guarantee a rodent-free home.

1. Full Home & Property Inspection

We conduct a top-to-bottom investigation to identify all current and potential rodent entry points, nesting areas, and damage. This includes the roof, attic, foundation, and crawl spaces.

2. Entry Point Sealing & Barriers

We use rodent-proof materials like steel wool, specialized caulks, and custom metal flashing to seal gaps around pipes, vents, siding, and utility lines—any opening larger than 1/4 inch.

3. Foundation & Burrow Barriers (Rat Slabbing)

To prevent rats from burrowing under your foundation or deck, we install a trench of wire mesh and concrete. This "rat slabbing" creates an impenetrable underground barrier.

4. Roof, Eave & Vent Protection

Roof rats are common in the Bay Area. We secure roof vents, junctions, and eaves with heavy-duty screens and materials that rodents can't chew through, blocking their primary highway into your attic.

5. Crawl Space Encapsulation & Securing

We secure crawl space vents and access doors, and can provide full encapsulation services to create a clean, dry, and rodent-proof barrier between the ground and your home's structure.

6. Decontamination & Odor Removal

After securing your home, we offer services to safely remove hazardous droppings, sanitize affected areas, and eliminate odors, restoring your home to a safe and healthy state.
